Harmonograph Mathematics

Mathematically a Harmonograph creates its images based on the movement of one or more damped pendulums with the following formulae:

\[ x(t) = A_1\sin(tf_1 + p_1) e^{-d_1t}) + A_2\sin(tf_2 + p_2) e^{-d_2t}\\ \\ y(t) = A_3\sin(tf_3 + p_3) e^{-d_3t}) + A_4\sin(tf_4 + p_4) e^{-d_4t} \] where \[ f = frequency, \\ p = phase, \\ A = amplitude, \\ d = damping, \\t = time \]
